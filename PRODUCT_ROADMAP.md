## üéØ Strategic Vision
To create the world's most transparent and engaging real-time map of the movement to end child marriage, empowering field partners to report success and donors to visualize impact.

## üìç Q1 2026: Foundation & Field Launch
**Theme: "The Infrastructure of Action"**
*   **Goal:** Establish a secure, high-fidelity portal for global activity reporting and visualization.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   [x] **Dual-Mode Visualization:** 3D Globe + 2D Flat Map for universal accessibility.
    *   [x] **Admin Approval Flow:** Managed moderation queue with advanced filtering (Search, Country, Type).
    *   [x] **Field Intake Portal:** Tally.so integration with dual-mode (manual/auto) geocoding.
    *   [x] **Premium UI Polish:** Boxicons integration, Custom Premium Selects, and Accessibility Contrast Fixes.
    *   [x] **Admin Rich Content:** Enable full Image and Markdown support in the Activity Editor.
    *   [ ] **Cloud Production Launch:** Full migration from local development to Supabase Cloud & Netlify.

## üìä Q2 2026: Impact Narrative & Analytics
**Theme: "From Data to Stories"**
*   **Goal:** Provide deeper insights into campaign progress through aggregate data and storytelling tools.
*   **Candidate Features:**
    *   **Live Leaderboards:** Tracking pledge growth by country/region.
    *   **Time-Series Visualization:** A "Timeline" mode to scrub back and see activity growth over the 100 days.
    *   **Regional Detail Pages:** Dedicated deep-dives for highly active regions (e.g., West Africa Cluster).
    *   **Impact Metrics v2:** Integration of "Lives Impacted" or "Community Reach" estimates based on pledge data.

## ü§ù Q3 2026: Community & Network Effect
**Theme: "Scaling the Movement"**
*   **Goal:** Enhance the platform's shareability and foster a sense of a global, connected community.
*   **Candidate Features:**
    *   **Social Impact Cards:** Generate auto-formatted images of activity markers for sharing on Instagram/X.
    *   **Partner Spotlight:** Ability for approved organizations to have "Verified" profiles on the map.
    *   **Direct Engagement Links:** "Donate to this event" or "Join this community group" buttons in the InfoPanel.
    *   **Multilingual Support:** Localization for French, Spanish, and Portuguese to better support global partners.
